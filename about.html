About Me

Hello! My name is Greg. I'm a content creator, musician, gamer, and aspiring law student. I'm a Morehouse College graduate with a Bachelor's Degree in History, and I've been making content ever since my Sophomore year of college. Since then, I have fallen in love with creating art, and sharing it with the world in my own humorous way. I’ve been a huge fan of video games for all of my life. Some of my favorite games growing up were Mario Kart Double Dash, Super Smash Bros. Melee, New Super Mario Bros, and Fire Emblem Awakening.

My love for music has been visible ever since I was toddler, banging pots and pans on the floor of my parent’s kitchen. I’d like to think that now, 23 years later, I’m able to make much more beautiful music. Whether it’s through the Oboe, Piano, or through FL Studio, I’m always trying to develop a new piece of music for listeners to enjoy. Some of my greatest musical inspirations are Mozart, Metro Boomin, DJ Mustard, and OmarCameUp.
